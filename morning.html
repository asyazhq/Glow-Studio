Your Perfect Morning Routine

❮ ❯

Step 1:

Cleanse


Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser that's formulated for oily skin. Look for a product that contains salicylic acid or benzoyl peroxide to help control oil production.

Step 2:

Moisturize


Even if you have oily skin, it's important to moisturize to keep your skin hydrated and healthy. Choose a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer that won't clog your pores.

❮ ❯
❮ ❯

Step 3:

Sunscreen


Finish your morning routine with a broad-spectrum sunscreen with an SPF of at least 30. Look for a product that's oil-free and non-comedogenic to avoid clogging your pores.
